NUTRITIONAL INFORMATION

1) IMPORTANCE OF HAVING A HEARTY BREAKFAST : Starting off with the first and the most neglected meal of the day, eating a nutritious breakfast can jumpstart your metabolism, helping your body burn calories more efficiently throughout the day and results in better cognitive functioning as it provides the fuel your brain needs to think, concentrate, and be productive.
Skipping breakfast can lead to overeating later in the day and weight gain ; eating a nutritious breakfast can help regulate hunger and reduce cravings. A healthy breakfast can provide the energy and nutrients needed to improve physical performance, especially for athletes and active individuals.Breakfast can help improve mood, energy levels, and overall well-being.
It's recommended to choose a balanced breakfast that includes whole grains, protein, and healthy fats to provide sustained energy and nutrients throughout the morning.

2) OMEGA 3 FATTY ACIDS AND CHIA SEEDS : Don't get the hype around the chia seed pudding and smoothie bowls? well its not just on there for the looks. Chia seeds and flaxseeds are two plant-based sources of omega-3 fatty acids.
They are also high in fiber, protein, and other essential nutrients.
They are rich in antioxidants, which protect against cell damage and reduce the risk of chronic diseases. Both are high in fiber, which can help support digestive health. They are alsp low in calories and high in fiber, making them an ideal food for weight management.

Omega-3 fatty acids are essential fatty acids that play a vital role in human health :
They have been shown to reduce the risk of heart disease by decreasing blood pressure and reducing inflammation. Omega-3 fatty acids are important for brain health and development, and have been linked to improved memory, concentration, and mood. They can help reduce inflammation and improve joint health.
This might not look like a major inclusion, but in the long run it can be very beneficial.

3) FIBRE AND FIBROUS FOODS : Fibre is an important component of a healthy diet and is found in fibrous fruits. Fibre helps to keep you feeling full for longer, which helps to regulate appetite and reduce the risk of obesity. It is also helpful in lowering cholesterol levels and reduce the risk of heart disease.
Fibre helps in the digestion process, which helps to keep your digestive system healthy. Fibre can also help to control your blood sugar levels, which can be beneficial for people with diabetes. Eating a diet rich in fruits and vegetables is one of the best ways to get your daily fibre intake. Eating fibrous fruits such as apples, pears, oranges, bananas and berries can provide you with a good source of fibre.
To maximize the benefits of fibre, it is important to eat a variety of fibrous fruits and vegetables each day. Eating a diet rich in fibre can help to reduce the risk of many health problems, so be sure to include plenty of fibrous fruits and vegetables in your daily diet.

4) CONSUMING FRESH FRUIT JUICES INSTEAD OF PACKAGED ALTERANTIVES : Used to buying tetra packs of excessively sweet fruit juices? You might want to reconsider your choices. Fresh fruit juices are a great way to get your daily dose of vitamins and minerals while also satisfying your thirst. Fresh fruit juices have the added benefit of being naturally sweet and don’t contain any artificial sweeteners that can be found in some packaged juices. They are also free of added sugar, preservatives and other additives. They are immediately made after you press the fruit, so they are packed with vitamins and minerals that have not had time to degrade. This means that you get all the nutritional benefits that the fruit has to offer.
Fresh fruit juices also give you more control over what goes into your drink, so you can create a variety of juices that are tailored to your individual tastes. Packaged juices may contain added sugar, preservatives and other additives that can reduce the nutritional value of the juice. In addition, the packaging process can also degrade the vitamins and minerals in the juice, meaning that you don’t get the full nutritional benefit from the juice.
Overall, fresh fruit juices provide more nutritional benefits than packaged juices and give you more control over what goes into your drink.

5) DO NOT CONSIDER CARBOHYDRATES AS YOUR ENEMY : Carbohydrates are an important part of a healthy diet. They are the body's main source of energy, providing fuel for physical activity and helping to keep the brain and nervous system functioning properly. Carbohydrates also play an important role in regulating blood sugar levels and providing essential dietary fibre.
Eating a variety of carbohydrates can help to ensure that you get the necessary vitamins and minerals that your body needs. It is important to choose complex carbohydrates such as whole grains, fruits and vegetables, which are digested more slowly and provide sustained energy. Simple carbohydrates such as white bread, cakes and sugary drinks should be limited to maintain a healthy diet.
Eating a diet that includes a variety of carbohydrates can help to provide your body with the energy and nutrients it needs while promoting overall health.
